FAQ

What does 'Quimble' stand for?
Nothing really. It rolled off the tongue well when a few friends were sitting around a college dining hall table coming up with name for a web startup.
Why would I ever come here?
Because you're tired of scouring the Internet to find that one feature from some software buried under many menus, button presses, and windows. You're tired of searching through countless Internet forums and crowd answer websites to find a vague answer that only sort of pertains to what you're looking for. Simply put, no one has time for all of that. Our search function will bring you to a page that will give you a series of screen shot with arrows telling you EXACTLY where to click/type to get what you need. If you don't know the exact name of the feature, then we've provided a en exhaustive search method that will allow you to narrow down to the exact feature in your piece of software.
